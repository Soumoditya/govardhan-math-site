The Eastern Seat of Vedic Wisdom

Around 507 BC, Acharya Adi Shankara — a very embodiment of Lord Shiva — descended upon this sacred land and established the four Amnaya Peethams to protect Sanatana Dharma for eternity.

Govardhan Math at Jagannath Puri presides over the eastern direction, associated with the Rigveda, the Mahavakya "Prajñānam Brahma", and the presiding deity Purushottam (Lord Jagannath). Padmapadacharya was appointed the first Shankaracharya.

145th Shankaracharya

Swami Nischalananda Saraswati

Born 30th June 1943 in Haripur Bakshi Tol, Madhubani, Bihar. His father Pandit Sri Lalvamshi Jha was a court vidwan of the Darbhanga Kingdom. Coronated as the 145th Shankaracharya on 9th February 1992.

He has delivered discourses at IIT Kanpur, IIM Kolkata, IIM Ahmedabad, ISRO, DRDO, BARC, AIIMS, and the Supreme Court. He advised ISRO scientists for the Chandrayaan-2 Moon Mission using Vedic Mathematics.
