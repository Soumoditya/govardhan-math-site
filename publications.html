The Official Publishing House

Swasti Prakashan Sansthan is the official publication wing of Govardhan Math, dedicated to disseminating works on Vedanta, Vedic Mathematics, Sanskrit literature, and the spiritual discourses of the Shankaracharya.

The 145th Shankaracharya Swami Nischalananda Saraswati has personally authored 11 well-known books on Vedic Mathematics for enrichment of this ancient knowledge.
